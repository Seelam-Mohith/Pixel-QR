About Us

Pixel QR is a modern QR code platform designed to make creating, customizing, and managing QR codes simple and accessible. Whether you need QR codes for websites, business cards, events, menus, or marketing campaigns, Pixel QR helps you generate professional, high-quality QR codes in just a few clicks.

Our mission is to provide a fast, reliable, and user-friendly experience with powerful customization options and high-resolution downloads for both personal and professional use.
